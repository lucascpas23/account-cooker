# Responsible use

This repository is defensive privacy research and local infrastructure. It must not be used for wash trading, fake market volume, market manipulation, governance manipulation, referral farming, airdrop farming, NFT wash trading, bridge abuse, uncontrolled dust spam, attacks on third-party programs, stolen or third-party keys, or evasion of legal obligations.

Only operate accounts and funds you are authorized to control. Prefer virtual time and Surfpool. Review protocol terms and local law before any external use. Do not represent activity as organic users, cryptographic anonymity, audited security, or guaranteed unlinkability.

The adapter API deliberately cannot accept arbitrary raw transactions. Program, action, RPC, cost, and rate allowlists belong in the central policy. New adapters require explicit safety classification and review.
